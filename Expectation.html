What to expect from Moringa

If you are considering a career transition to tech then definitely Moringa School should be your pick. Moringa School is an intensive coding bootcamp that arms new developers with the skills they need to move into the tech workforce. Moringa School requires one to be fully committed to the program, it is time consuming so if you're thinking about joining, you should know beforehand what you will and won't get out of it.

What you should expect

  1. At Moringa you will change your perspective. You will need to cultivate flexibility and adaptability inorder to excel.
  2. Moringa is led by thoughtful, experienced and motivated technical mentors. Moringa School is very demanding for both students and Technical Mentors, however, here you will receive much one-on-one time with your Technical Mentors and pair programmers.
  3. At Moringa you will learn an astounding amount of material. One can absorb an incredible amount of information when they dedicate themselves to it daily. You will likely spend much of your time after class watching videos on coding also workking on your Independent Projects during the weekends and you'll stretch your mind in ways you never thought possible.
  4. Moringa offers learning flexibility to its students. In this tech era we are seeing companiess allowing their workers to work remotely. Moringa uses this approach to teach learners how they can work remotely while maintaining teamwork with colleagues.

What you should not expect

    To learn everything. Even the computer science graduate doesn't have a complete mastery of the topics in that field. At Moringa you'll have the ability to put together pieces of web apllication and understand the infrastructure that hosts aplications and makes them accessible to the public.
